BEANS RECIPE

Description

Beans is an amazing meal for every Nigerian home this is made by cooking beans to a degree that it get so mashy. at this point you caqn decide to eat it all alone or get a nice bread to eat with it.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Water
  • grounded pepper
  • Onions
  • Maggi
  • Ground nut oil or palm oil

steps

  1. boil your beans for close to 1hour or 45mins as you desire
  2. add onions
  3. add your grounded pepper if you need to
  4. Add your maggi and an amount of salt suitable for the mixture
