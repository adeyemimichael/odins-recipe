JOLLOF RICE RECIPE

Description

This is most pupolar dish in Nigeria, and it is common in almost every part of the country, it is a thing amisdt us. A quick way to make one

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • grounded pepper
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Curry and thyme flavour
  • Maggi
  • Ground nut oil

steps

  1. par boil your rice (determine the amount of rice measurement you want to cook) after par boiling for close to 15mins
  2. Sieve the rice and put it separate
  3. Pour your oil in the pot that is already on the fire and put your already sliced onions in it
  4. add your grouned pepper mixed with tomato paste and also add your curry and time
  5. Add your maggi and an amount of salt suitable for the mixture
  6. add enough water to allow the rice rinse well in the mixture add all other materials you like, like green beans and cray fish
  7. wait for anothere 20 - 25 minutes your jollof rice is ready
